Ans: Sensors detect physical parameters and convert them to electrical signals. They are the foundational perception layer capturing real-world data for digital processing.

Ans: Utilizing electromagnetic fields to automatically identify and track tags attached to objects. Crucial in IoT for asset tracking, inventory management, and access control.
